Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

The basic image to pixel art converter runs locally in your browser using HTML canvas. Your uploaded image is not sent to our server for the free local conversion flow.

Local image processing

When you use the free converter, the image file is handled by your browser. The tool uses canvas-based processing to create the pixel art preview and PNG download on your device.

Optional paid and AI flows

Optional AI restyling and paid checkout require a separate action. Payments are processed by Creem, and AI jobs may use a third-party AI provider after you explicitly start that flow.

We may use basic hosting logs provided by Vercel, such as request metadata, error logs, and security-related logs needed to operate the website.
