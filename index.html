FAQ

Is this image to pixel art converter free?

Yes. The core converter is free and runs in your browser, so you can turn images into pixel art without signing up.

Can I convert a photo to pixel art?

Yes. Upload a photo, choose a lower pixel art width for a chunkier look, or raise it to keep more facial and object detail.

Does the tool upload my image?

The basic converter does not upload your image. It runs locally with HTML canvas. Optional AI restyling would require a separate upload and consent step.

What image formats work?

The converter supports common browser image formats such as JPG, PNG, WebP, and GIF still frames.

How do I make 8-bit style pixel art?

Try the Sprite preset, choose the PICO-8 or NES inspired palette, and keep the color count low.

Can I use the PNG for games?

You can download the PNG and use it in mockups, prototypes, avatars, and game experiments. Check the rights of your original image.
